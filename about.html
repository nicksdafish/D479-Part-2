Welcome to Taniti

The Island

Taniti is a small tropical island in the Pacific known for its volcano, lush rainforests, and pristine beaches.

Culture & People

Home to a friendly, native population of 20,000, Taniti celebrates local traditions and welcomes visitors warmly.

Local Tips

Most young locals speak English. US dollars are accepted, and the island is safe with very low violent crime.

Electricity

120 volts (same as U.S.). Power adapters not usually required for American travelers.

Transportation

Rental cars, buses, bikes, and walking are all convenient ways to explore Taniti City and nearby areas.

Currency

USD, euros, and yen are accepted. Major credit cards welcome; currency exchange is widely available.
