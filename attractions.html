Top Attractions in Taniti

Volcano Hikes

Guided hikes to the active volcano offer incredible views and photo opportunities.

Rainforest Tours

Explore the island's dense tropical jungle with expert guides.

Sandy Beaches

Relax on white-sand beaches or snorkel along the reef. Many beaches are near Taniti City.

Fishing Charters

Join a half- or full-day fishing tour off the coast with local guides.

Zip-Lining

Fly through the trees in the Taniti rainforest. Great for thrill seekers and families alike.

Island Tours

Bus and boat tours are available daily and cover key landmarks and cultural spots.
