Plan Your Stay in Taniti

Accommodations

Options range from beach resorts and bed & breakfasts to private rentals and hostels.

Dining

Enjoy local seafood, American-style meals, or Pan-Asian cuisine at our 10+ restaurants.

Transportation

Get around with rental cars, hotel shuttles, or public buses. Bikes are also available to rent.

Helpful Tips

Plan ahead around national holidays as some attractions and restaurants close on those days.

Hospitals & Clinics

One hospital and several clinics provide quality care. Many staff members are multilingual.

Accessibility

Taniti City is flat and walkable; many sites are accessible for seniors and families with strollers.
